Best Places in Maui to eat!

  • Mamas Fish House

    • Mama's Fish has a reputaion as one of the most
    notable fine dining restaurants on Maui, specializing
    in fresh seafood and evoking a classic polynesian
    island aesthetic.



  • Star Noodle

    • Located on Front st. in Lahaina, Star Noodle is one
    of the most sought after restaurants by locals and
    tourist's! Offering up fresh noodles, sake cocktails
    and asian-inspired share plates.



  • Flatbread Company

    • Voted the best pizza on town! they make their pizza
    in an open-line kitchen with a wood-fired oven. The
    pizza comes on hot and delicious with a variety of
    available toppings.

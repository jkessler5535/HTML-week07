Best Places in Maui to eat!

Maui, the stunning Hawaiian island, offers not only breathtaking views but also and incredible culinary experience. With many restaurants featuring local ingredients, it's no suprise that Maui boasts some of the best dining options in Hawaii. From fresh seafood to farm-to-table dishes, visitors can enjoy a diverse range of food on the island. Some of the must-try favorites include Mama's Fish House, a seafood restaurant with breathtaking ocean view, Star Noodle, specializing in modern Asian cuisine, and Flatbread Comapany, serving delicious pizza. With so many incredible restaurants to choose from, Maui is undoubtedly a foodie's paradise.

  • Mamas Fish House

    • Mama's Fish has a reputaion as one of the most notable fine dining restaurants on Maui, specializing in fresh seafood and evoking a classic polynesian island aesthetic.

  • Star Noodle

    • Located on Front st. in Lahaina, Star Noodle is one of the most sought after restaurants by locals and tourist's! Offering up fresh noodles, sake cocktails and asian-inspired share plates.

  • Flatbread Company

    • Voted the best pizza on town! they make their pizza in an open-line kitchen with a wood-fired oven. The pizza comes on hot and delicious with a variety of available toppings.

Best Surf in Maui

  1. Makena Beach
  2. Pai Beach
  3. Kihei Beach

Maui Offers some of the best surfing in the world, with Makena Beach, Pai Beach, and Kihei Beach being some of the top spots.
These beaches offer consistent waves waves year-round, with Makena boasting 9 different surf breaks. If you're looking for incredible
scenery , consistant waves, and an unforgettable surfing experience, Maui is the place to be.
